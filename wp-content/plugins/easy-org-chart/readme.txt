= Get your Organizational Chart. =

Create a dazzling organizational chart in a snap with Easy Org Chart.

You’ll be able to easily build, person after person, your organizational chart.

The goal of the plugin is to let you configure all your persons, and build automatically the chart.

= How to use our plugin ? =

* You just have to download the plugin from the Wordpress Repo.
* Activate your plugin on your Dashboard.
* At this point you will see an Easy Org Chart tab, you just have to create a new Org Chart by completing the fields.
* Once created, go back to the list of your Org Chart and you will see a Shortcode.
* All you have to do, is put the Shortcode wherever you want to get your Organizational Chart.
